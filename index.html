Garton Jones Real Estate is a leading residential sales, letting and property management agent located in Central London.

We are a well respected estate agent renowned for offering a large but versatile portfolio of luxury properties for both sale and to rent.

With boutique style offices located across the city in keys areas such as Westminster, SW1, Chelsea Bridge Wharf and Nine Elms, SW8, Albert Embankment, SE1 and Royal Arsenal, SE18 we offer a bespoke, informal but totally professional service to suit all buyers and prospective tenants.

We also offer our customers unrivalled expertise and knowledge of the Central London property market based on our many years of experience.
